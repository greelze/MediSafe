MediSafe Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, username during registration.
  • Sensor Data: Temperature, humidity, UV index readings from connected devices.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with the platform, settings preferences, alert history.
  • Location Data: Facility locations for sensor deployment (not geolocation tracking).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide core monitoring and alerting services
  • Generate analytics reports and predictions
  • Send threshold violation notifications
  • Improve service functionality and reliability
  • Communicate important account updates

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers for hosting, analytics, and support (bound by confidentiality)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business account administrators (within your organization)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit)
  • Supabase PostgreSQL with row-level security
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Automatic backups with 7-day retention

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Export your sensor data history

Contact support@medisafe.com to exercise these rights.

6. Sensor Data Retention

Sensor readings retained for 90 days by default. Account admins may configure custom retention periods up to 2 years.

7. Children's Privacy

MediSafe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email and in-app notifications. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions: privacy@medisafe.com
